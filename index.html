FAQ

How long does it take to make a website?

Depending on the job it can take as little as one day to one week for larger projects. We always give clients a fixed time estimate

Do you offer other services?

Yes! We offer everything to need to get your site up and running, including hosting. We also offer ongoing plans where we take care information and content updates for those who prefer to be hands off. We also offer a CMS (content management system) for clients who'd like to update their site regularly, for example if you create blog posts or plan to post content regularly. Please see our plans below for more info

Are there any hidden charges?

None! We believe that prices shouldn't be secretive or taken out of the clouds. All of our pricing is transparent in our plans section. If you require a custom plan we will agree a custom quote with you. We also support charities and non-profits by offering a 20% discount on all services
